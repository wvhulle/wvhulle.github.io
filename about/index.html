Professional

I am a mathematician and computer scientist from Ghent, Belgium. I attempted physics, but became enamoured with fermenting bacteria before finishing. Since 2020, I have worked in scientific and tech start-ups as a software engineer. In 2025, I gathered my experience and taught a Rust course for intermediate developers.

In the past year I broadened my horizon to include C++ in my expertise and help companies deal with their large legacy codebases.

For a more complete overview, see my CV.
